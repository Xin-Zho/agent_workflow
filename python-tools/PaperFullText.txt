Sample Material: PEO/AgNW Composite
Preparation: Dissolve PEO in deionized water at 5 wt%.
Add AgNW dispersion and sonicate for 30 min at 100W.
Cast film and cure at 80 degC for 2h.
Results: Electrical conductivity ~5000 S/cm (Figure 3).
Tensile strength 15 MPa (Table 3).
Table 2: Composition - 25 wt% AgNW in PEO matrix.
